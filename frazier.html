Frazier-Simplex
Communication Design
2015
I redesigned assorted documents, including posters, brochures, for a small engineering firm in Pennsylvannia named Frazier-Simplex. My aim in the overall design of all materials was to create something very simple that highlights the key features and strengths of the company, primarily their machines and equipment. I also worked with one of the employees to create a Chinese version of the brochures, since Frazier-Simplex works with many other companies in China.
