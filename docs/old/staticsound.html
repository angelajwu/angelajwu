STATIC SOUND APPAREL
Web Design / Fashion Design / Marketing
2014 - Present
Static Sound Apparel is an online based company inspired mainly by music and pop-culture, established in the summer of 2014.

I currently work as the founder and designer, drawing graphics either by hand or digitally, managing emails and social media sites to ensure all customers are satisfied. I also work with contemporary photographers on Instagram to market my brand.
