WORD MAGAZINE
Magazine Design
2012 - 2014
WORD Magazine is a student-led magazine from students at Shanghai American School Pudong, published quarterly.

I served as the layout editor of WORD magazine for two years. As layout editor, I worked with other students, including the editor and chief of the magazine, and our two teacher sponsors to create and release issues. I was responsible for assigning layouts to other students who were a part of the layout staff, consolidating these layouts from other students, creating my own layouts for articles, and adding finishing touches such as page numbers, the table of contents, and the title page.
