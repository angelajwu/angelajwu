ABOUT


I'm a designer and a creator.

I'm currently studying civil engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, intending to double major in Human Computer Interaction with a focus on design. I'm interested in the way humans interact with their environments, both physically through their interaction with physical structures, and mentally, through their interaction with digital systems.

I love trying new things and creating new projects for myself, whether it be creating a clothing company, exploring cities, or founding a photo magazine. I'm also an avid lover of nature, music, and cooking.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
