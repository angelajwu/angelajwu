"Pipes & Shrooms" - Senior Capstone Project
October - December 2017

In collaboration with Emmett Horton, Daniel Ha, Roseanne Usnay, and Jason Yoon
The objective of this project was to design, construct and build a structure to help Mario and Luigi cross a 12 foot lava pit. The structure could only be attached to 4 concrete columns, at least 2 feet from the lava. On test day, teams had 20 minutes to erect their structure, ensuring no part of it touches the lava. Teams were given $200 and approximately 6 weeks to complete the project.


Sample of CAD Drawings of Final Design

In addition to plan and elevation views of the structure (above), detailed CAD drawings of the attachments and bracings were also produced.

Almost 40 pages of supporting calculations were done using MathCAD to ensure the structure would bear the load of Mario or Luigi (assumed to be approximately 180 lbs).

Poster Presentation of Final Design

I created the poster design for our team's final presentation. The design incorporates our created structure (a rainbow bridge) into a themed poster that is nostalgic of the 8-bit Super Mario Odyssey.

Structure Tested by Mario & Luigi

Our final structure cost $172.36, weighed 76.9 lbs, and was constructed in less than 11 minutes.
